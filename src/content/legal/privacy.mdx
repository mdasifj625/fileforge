# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-13

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to File Forge. We built this platform with a single core principle: your data belongs to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## 2. Zero Server Processing
Unlike traditional online file converters, File Forge operates on a **local-first architecture**. When you select a file (image, video, audio, or document) to be processed, it is loaded directly into your browser's local memory (RAM).

- We do not upload your files to our servers.
- We do not have access to your files.
- We cannot view, share, or sell your data.

## 3. Data Collection
We believe in collecting the absolute minimum amount of data required to run our service.

- **Files:** Never collected.
- **Analytics:** We may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand general website traffic and feature usage. This data is fully anonymized.
- **Accounts:** If you choose to create an account, we store your email address and authentication credentials securely using our authentication provider.

## 4. Cookies and Local Storage
File Forge utilizes your browser's LocalStorage and IndexedDB to cache WebAssembly binaries, AI models, and your personal app preferences. This allows the app to load faster and function entirely offline. These caches remain on your device and are not transmitted to us.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please visit our [Contact Page](/contact).
