## How to Remove Backgrounds from Images

Removing the background from your images has never been faster or more secure. File Forge utilizes a state-of-the-art **Local AI Model (BEN2)** that runs entirely inside your web browser. This means your images are **never uploaded to any server**, guaranteeing absolute privacy.

### Step-by-step Guide:

1. **Upload your image:** Drag and drop your image directly into the center canvas, or click to upload.
2. **Select the AI Tool:** Click on the "Remove Background" tool located in the right properties panel.
3. **Wait for Processing:** File Forge will securely download the AI model directly into your browser's local cache (first time only) and seamlessly extract the foreground subject.
4. **Refine the Edges (Optional):** Use the advanced Edge Feathering and Edge Shifting sliders to non-destructively soften or contract the mask borders for a perfect cutout.
5. **Export:** Click the "Export" button in the top bar to securely download your high-resolution PNG with a transparent background.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Is this background removal tool really free?

Yes! Because the AI processing happens entirely on your own device's hardware, we don't have to pay expensive server costs. We pass those savings directly to you.

### What happens to my images after I upload them?

Nothing is uploaded. File Forge is a **local-first application**. Your files are read into your browser's memory and processed by your own CPU/GPU. Once you close the tab, your data vanishes.

### What is Edge Feathering and Edge Shifting?

Edge feathering blurs the hard edge of the AI cutout, which is extremely useful for blending subjects with hair or fur into new backgrounds. Edge shifting contracts or expands the mask inward/outward to remove lingering background halos.

### Can I restore parts of the background that the AI mistakenly removed?

Yes! Our non-destructive WebGL masking engine allows you to select the "Mask Brush" tool. You can manually paint to Restore or Erase specific portions of the AI-generated mask in real-time.
